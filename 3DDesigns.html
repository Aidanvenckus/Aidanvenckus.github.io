Here you will find the 3D models I've designed as STL files, 3D Prints, and Animations. I designed most of my models recently in Fusion360 but in the past used SolidWorks.

I choose to have the STL files (Standard Triangle Language) avaliable as they are the industry standard file type for 3D Printing. STL uses a series of triangles to represent the surfaces of a model then, applications such as CURA allow it to become Gcode (Geometic code) which are the machine's instructions for pathing/moving/speed/etc. for use in a 3D printer.
